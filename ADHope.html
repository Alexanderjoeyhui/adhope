About Us

Brothers Alf & David Hope established the company in 1917, as young and budding businessmen they sold fur products to the young & wealthy. Since then the business has re-invented itself.

In the 1980s the business turned its focus to leather & suede products and now A&D Hope are proud to specialise in the design, sourcing, manufacture and supply of men's tailoring. The business continues to be run by David's Grandson - Jeffrey Hope.

The highest standards with attention to detail.
